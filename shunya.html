Shunya, Energy chain challenge

A DeFi app build for transparent trading of energy and carbon credit tokens. Directly connects users who have extra energy (like solar producers) with people who need energy. Tracks real-time energy use with smart devices (like electricity meters). Makes carbon credit trading fair and automated. Blockchain ensures an immutable ledger for verifying carbon credits and transactions, eliminating double spending. Ensures privacy for business-sensitive data using ZK-SNARKs (a privacy tech).xt
This was a group made project in which I mainly contributed in ideation as well as frontend development of the app interface
